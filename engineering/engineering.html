Flying the 3DR Solo

I am a fourth-year Electrical Engineering student at the University of Hawaii at Manoa.

I was a part of Dr. Yingfei Dong's Vertically Integrated Project (VIP) for my EEx96. As a sophomore, I used Kali Linux to learn and test Wifi penetration techniques, in regards to different flaws in encryption and network protocol. We then applied wireless penetration and traffic analysis techniques to UAS. For my senior project, we used Python to produce statistics and analyze trends in wireless network-based drone communication, and we successful were able to distinguished two drones from each other by looking at the trends in their respective network communication.
